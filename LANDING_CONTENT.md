## SECTION 3: How it Works (Steps)

**Badge:** How it works

**Titre:** An end-to-end solution for lead generation

**Sous-titre:** With LeadSwap, you describe your ideal customer once and get verified, enriched leads ready for outreach.

### Step 01 - Describe your ICP (Active)
Tell the agent who you're looking for in plain language: "Find SaaS startups in Berlin that use Stripe and are hiring salespeople."

### Step 02 - Agent hunts the web
LeadSwap uses neural search to find matching companies, then visits each site to verify and extract data.

### Step 03 - Review & refine
See your leads with relevance scores. Give feedback to improve future searches.

### Step 04 - Export qualified leads
Download your verified lead list as CSV/JSON, ready for your CRM or outreach tool.
